Formula

Given a matrix \(A\), vector \(b\), and initial guess \(x_0\), the method begins with:

\[ r_0 = b - Ax_0 \] \[ p_0 = r_0 \] \[ \alpha_k = \frac{r_k^T r_k}{p_k^T A p_k} \] \[ x_{k+1} = x_k + \alpha_k p_k \] \[ r_{k+1} = r_k - \alpha_k A p_k \] \[ \beta_k = \frac{r_{k+1}^T r_{k+1}}{r_k^T r_k} \] \[ p_{k+1} = r_{k+1} + \beta_k p_k \]

Worked Example 1

Solve the system \(Ax = b\), where:

\[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix}, \quad b = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}, \quad x_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 1: Compute the Initial Residual

\[ r_0 = b - Ax_0 \] \[ Ax_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ p_0 = r_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 2: First Iteration

\[ Ap_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_0^T r_0 = 1^2 + 2^2 = 5 \] \[ p_0^T Ap_0 = (1)(6) + (2)(7) = 20 \] \[ \alpha_0 = \frac{5}{20} = 0.25 \] \[ x_1 = x_0 + \alpha_0 p_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + 0.25 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0.25 \\ 0.5 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_1 = r_0 - \alpha_0 Ap_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} - 0.25 \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ 7 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -0.5 \\ 0.25 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 3: Compute the Next Direction

\[ r_1^T r_1 = (-0.5)^2 + (0.25)^2 = 0.3125 \] \[ \beta_0 = \frac{r_1^T r_1}{r_0^T r_0} = \frac{0.3125}{5} = 0.0625 \] \[ p_1 = r_1 + \beta_0 p_0 = \begin{bmatrix} -0.5 \\ 0.25 \end{bmatrix} + 0.0625 \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -0.4375 \\ 0.375 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 4: Second Iteration

\[ Ap_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 1 \\ 1 & 3 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} -0.4375 \\ 0.375 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -1.375 \\ 0.6875 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ p_1^T Ap_1 = (-0.4375)(-1.375) + (0.375)(0.6875) = 0.859375 \] \[ \alpha_1 = \frac{0.3125}{0.859375} = 0.363636 \] \[ x_2 = x_1 + \alpha_1 p_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 0.25 \\ 0.5 \end{bmatrix} + 0.363636 \begin{bmatrix} -0.4375 \\ 0.375 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0.090909 \\ 0.636364 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_2 = r_1 - \alpha_1 Ap_1 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \]

Since the residual is now zero, the method has converged. Therefore, the solution is approximately:

\[ x = \begin{bmatrix} 0.090909 \\ 0.636364 \end{bmatrix} \]

Worked Example 2

Solve the system \(Ax = b\), where:

\[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix}, \quad b = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix}, \quad x_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 1: Compute the Initial Residual

\[ r_0 = b - Ax_0 \] \[ Ax_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} - \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ p_0 = r_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} \]

Step 2: First Iteration

\[ Ap_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 & 0 \\ 0 & 2 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_0^T r_0 = 2^2 + 4^2 = 20 \] \[ p_0^T Ap_0 = (2)(4) + (4)(8) = 40 \] \[ \alpha_0 = \frac{20}{40} = 0.5 \] \[ x_1 = x_0 + \alpha_0 p_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} + 0.5 \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \] \[ r_1 = r_0 - \alpha_0 Ap_0 = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 4 \end{bmatrix} - 0.5 \begin{bmatrix} 4 \\ 8 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \\ 0 \end{bmatrix} \]

Since the residual is zero after the first iteration, the method has converged. Therefore, the solution is:

\[ x = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix} \]
